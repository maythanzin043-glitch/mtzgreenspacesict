Problems Without Green Spaces

Without green spaces, cities become hotter, more polluted, and less healthy for people. When they are missing, urban areas face serious environmental and social problems.

The absence of green spaces can lead to increased air pollution, urban heat island effect, and higher stress levels among people. Wildlife also loses habitats, and cities become less sustainable and less enjoyable to live in.
